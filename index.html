SVR.JS is discontinued

We want to thank all our users and supporters of SVR.JS. After careful consideration, we have decided to discontinue SVR.JS. This decision allows us to focus on the development of Ferron, a fast and memory-safe web server written in Rust, and to optimize our infrastructure costs.

SVR.JS has been an incredible journey, and we truly appreciate the community that has formed around it. If you're looking for a high-performance web server, we encourage you to check out Ferron, which represents our vision for the future of web server technology.

Thank you for being part of this project. We appreciate your support and look forward to building even better solutions together.
